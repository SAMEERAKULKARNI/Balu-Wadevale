Our Journey

Balu Wadewale started as a humble street cart in Talegaon, serving hot and spicy vada pav to locals. With dedication, authentic flavors, and a growing loyal customer base, we've expanded to become one of the most beloved food spots in the area. Our mission remains the same — serve fresh, affordable, and delicious Maharashtrian snacks with a smile.

Vadapav

Spicy batata vada in soft pav, served with chutney & fried chili. A street food favorite.

Samosa

Golden crispy outer layer filled with flavorful mashed potato and spice mix.

Moong Bhaji

Crunchy moong dal fritters served hot — the perfect rainy day snack!
